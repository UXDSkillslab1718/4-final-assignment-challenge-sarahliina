Programming Bootcamp

Introduction to Processing

The programming bootcamp aimed to teach us knowledge and understanding of the logic and structure required in programming. It was focused on stimulating our creativity and validating our concepts. It was not intended to teach us specific programming skills, like front or backend development, but to create programmed sketches and prototypes. This as a technique will help us to validate design solutions and communicate resulting products. Each of the five days of this bootcamp conveyed new knowledge about fundamental programming structure, which we then had to implement in the project of the day.
